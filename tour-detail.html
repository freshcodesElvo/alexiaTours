Maasai Mara Safari

Experience the magic of the Great Migration and the majestic Big Five in their natural habitat.

Tour Itinerary

A day-by-day breakdown of your unforgettable adventure.

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi & Transfer to Maasai Mara

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) where you will be met by our representative. Enjoy a scenic flight to the Maasai Mara National Reserve. After settling into your lodge, embark on an afternoon game drive.

Day 2: Full Day Game Drive & Cultural Visit

Spend the full day exploring the Mara's vast plains. In the afternoon, you have the option to visit a Maasai village to learn about their culture.
